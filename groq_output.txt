To achieve the perfect roasted cherry tomatoes, follow these steps:

Wash and dry the cherry tomatoes using a gentle blotting motion with a clean kitchen towel to remove excess moisture. Then, toss them in a bowl with 2 tablespoons of olive oil, a pinch of salt, and a few grinds of black pepper, using a gentle folding motion to combine the ingredients evenly.

Next, spread the tomatoes in a single layer on a light-colored, parchment-lined baking sheet, leaving some space between each tomato for air circulation. Roast them in the oven for 15-20 minutes, or until they are softened and slightly blistered, checking on them every 5 minutes to ensure they don't overcook.

Finally, remove the tomatoes from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving. They should be tender, caramelized, and bursting with flavor.
To prepare the pesto, start by selecting 1/2 to 3/4 cup of fresh basil leaves and 1/4 cup of pine nuts. Peel and chop 2 cloves of garlic, and add a pinch of salt to bring out the flavors. Combine these ingredients in a food processor and pulse until they are finely chopped, stopping to scrape down the sides as needed. Add 1-2 tablespoons of olive oil and continue pulsing until the mixture reaches a smooth, creamy paste. Monitor the consistency and adjust as needed, adding more olive oil if the pesto is too thick or more basil or pine nuts if it's too thin.
